CARDS

Card Description Additional Notes

Attack

 Ends your turn without drawing a card. Forces the next player to take two turns

Diffuse

 Secretly put your last drawn card back ino the draw pile There are three diffuses in the game: one for each player and one drawable diffuse in the deck.

Draw from Bottom

 End your turn by drawing the card at the bottom of the draw pile! Used if you know that the exploding kitten is the top card.

Exploding Kittens

 Show the card immediately If you draw this card and cannot use a diffuse, you lose the game.

Favor

 One player must give you a card of their choice.

Reverse

 Reverse the order of play and end your turn without drawing a card

Shuffle

 Shuffles the draw pile Useless if there is only one card left (the exploding kitten)

Skip

 Ends your turn without drawing a card Exploding Kitten
